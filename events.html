Upcoming Events

Join us for exciting chess events at the Broad Run High School Chess Club! From weekly meetings to countywide tournaments, there’s something for players of all skill levels. Our events are held at Broad Run High School, 21670 Ashburn Rd, Ashburn, VA 20147, unless otherwise noted. Check out our calendar below and sign up for tournaments to compete and connect with fellow chess enthusiasts!

Highlights include our Weekly Chess Meetings every Wednesday at 4:18 PM in Room 117 and several outreach activities. Don’t miss out on workshops, casual play sessions, and special events designed to enhance your skills and love for the game.
