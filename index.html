Welcome to the BRHS Chess Club!

Join us at the Broad Run High School Chess Club, where strategy meets community! Located at 21670 Ashburn Rd, Ashburn, VA 20147, our club is a vibrant hub for chess enthusiasts of all skill levels attending Broad Run. Whether you're a beginner learning the basics or a seasoned player aiming to refine your tactics, we offer a welcoming environment to grow, compete, and connect.

Our meetings take place every Wednesday at 4:18 PM in Ms. Nafey's Room, room 117, providing a consistent space to practice and engage with fellow players. We pride ourselves on fostering a supportive atmosphere where members can challenge themselves through friendly matches, participate in workshops, and prepare for tournaments. Last semester, we hosted over 10 meetups and celebrated a 1st Place victory at the LightRidge High School Chess Tournament!

Chess is more than a game—it's a way to sharpen your mind, build lasting friendships, and develop critical thinking skills that extend beyond the board. At BRHS, we're committed to making chess accessible and enjoyable for our students. Come visit us, explore our events, or reach out at broadrunchessclub@gmail.com to learn more about how you can get involved!

Join the BRHS Chess Club Today!

Ready to make your move? The BRHS Chess Club invites you to join our community of passionate players at 21670 Ashburn Rd, Ashburn, VA 20147. Our club meets every Wednesday at 4:18 PM in Room 117, offering a perfect opportunity to dive into the world of chess. Whether you're looking to compete in tournaments, enjoy casual games, or learn from experienced players, we have something for everyone.
