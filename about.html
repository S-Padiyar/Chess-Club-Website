Our Story

The BRHS Chess Club was established in 2015 at Briar Woods High School, located at 21670 Ashburn Rd, Ashburn, VA 20148. What began as a small group of chess enthusiasts has grown into a vibrant community dedicated to fostering strategic thinking, sportsmanship, and friendship. Our mission is to make chess accessible to all students, regardless of experience, and to provide a supportive environment for growth and competition.

Over the years, we’ve celebrated milestones like our 1st Place win at the 2025 Regional High School Chess Championship. Our weekly meetings, held every Wednesday at 3:30 PM in Room 105, are a cornerstone of our club, offering opportunities for practice, learning, and connection. We’re proud to be a part of the Briar Woods community and look forward to continuing our legacy of excellence.

Our Impact

The BRHS Chess Club has made a lasting impact at Briar Woods High School and beyond. By fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork, we’ve helped over 50 members this year develop skills that extend far beyond the chessboard. Our club has hosted over 10 workshops and tournaments, including our signature Regional High School Chess Championship, where we secured 1st Place in 2025.

We’re proud to spotlight members like Alex J., who ranks in the top 5 nationally in the U18 division, inspiring others to pursue excellence. Through community outreach, such as free chess clinics for local middle schools, we’re spreading the love of chess and building connections in Ashburn, VA. Join us to be part of this dynamic community!
